Our Story

From a single counter to Dinanagar's trusted electrical shop

Shri Param Hans Electrical And Electronics began as a small electrical counter in Shiv Market, G.T. Road — started with a simple belief: sell only genuine products, and treat every customer's home wiring as carefully as our own.

Over the years, word of mouth turned that small counter into a full-range electrical and electronics store, now trusted by families, contractors, shopkeepers, and institutions across Dinanagar and nearby areas. We became authorized dealers for brands like Philips, Polycab, Jaquar, Goldmedal and KEI — not by accident, but because we insisted on stocking only what we could vouch for.

Our Mission

To make genuine, brand-authorized electrical products accessible and affordable to every home and business in Dinanagar, backed by honest advice and dependable after-sales support.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted electrical and electronics store in the region — known not just for what we sell, but for how honestly we sell it.
